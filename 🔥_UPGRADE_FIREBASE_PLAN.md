## 🎯 How to Upgrade (5 minutes)

### **Step 1: Go to Firebase Console**

Click this link:
**https://console.firebase.google.com/project/foodbridge-app-51332/usage/details**

### **Step 2: Click "Upgrade Project"**

You'll see a button to upgrade to Blaze plan.

### **Step 3: Add Payment Method**

- Enter your credit card details
- **Don't worry**: You won't be charged unless you exceed free tier
- Set up billing alerts (recommended)

### **Step 4: Set Budget Alert (Recommended)**

1. After upgrading, go to **"Usage and billing"**
2. Click **"Details & settings"**
3. Click **"Set budget alert"**
4. Set alert at **$5** or **$10**
5. You'll get email if you approach this amount

### **Step 5: Confirm Upgrade**

Click **"Continue"** to complete the upgrade.
